About me

Hey! You probably didn't guess by the name, but I am a huge nerd about Saab cars, among other things.
I enjoy photography (I try to take my little praktica everywhere I go), cars, music, exploring new places and spending time with my friends.
Pretty basic sounding, I know, but it's a start.

I think taking photos and writing little posts is a great way to document and remember things later on in life.
